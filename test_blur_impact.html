Testing blurriness impact

  1. Do not make significant eye movements,
    blink or look anywhere else except for
    the black dot in the center
  2. Make sure that the black dot in the
    center is exactly at your eye level
  3. The distance between you eyes and
    the monitor should be the arm's length
  4. In the begining place your mouse on the
    Start button, but don't press it yet
  5. Start concentrating on the black dot and
    at the same time press the Start button
  6. When the peripheral stimulus disappear
    press the Start button again (it will switch
    to Stop button automaticaly)
    • If the stimulus did not disappear
      or you have blinked or moved your
      eyes - press Start and start over
  7. Press Save
